Customized Google Maps for Auslandsdienst:
It was my job to transfer and visualize the data on a Google Map and a construct a list with details about each place. Expanding a WP plugin (built by my predecessor) I also added the creation of a new database for a new app displaying a table of past and future conferences.

Airforce ROTC Inventory Excel:

This significantly simplified inventory checks and changes. I can send you the sample xlsm file upon request.
