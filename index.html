Background and Philosophy:
Born in Austria, I was fortunate to complete high school in the United States, and spend time on the West Coast, East Coast and in the Midwest. At University the idea of value driven production and design impacted me deeply. Professional as well as personally I aim to never create a solution to a non-existent problem. And I don’t think that can be said for everyone. My four college level courses in 2D/3D Design, Figure Drawing and Portrait Drawing developed my artistic side and I believe that value can come from many places. Transferring Universities has been challenging in many ways but I’ve never been happier with what direction my life is taking.
